# Trenuj Ratuj

> AI-powered medical emergency training platform for paramedic students and healthcare professionals in Poland.

Live URL: https://trenujratuj.pl

## About

Production platform providing realistic medical scenarios where students practice critical decision-making — cardiac emergencies, trauma, respiratory distress, neurological and pediatric cases — with real-time AI feedback. All protocols aligned to ERC 2021 Guidelines.

## Key Features

- AI-powered scenario engine covering 8+ medical specialties
- Real-time adaptive feedback based on student decisions
- ABCDE, SAMPLE, CPR, ACLS and medication administration training
- Progress tracking and performance analytics dashboard
- Scenario builder for medical instructors
- Role-based access: students, instructors, administrators
- Multi-tier subscription with institutional licensing
- GDPR-compliant with JWT authentication

## Tech Stack

React 18 · Vite · Node.js · Express.js · PostgreSQL · Redis · Tailwind CSS · shadcn/ui · Docker · Heroku
